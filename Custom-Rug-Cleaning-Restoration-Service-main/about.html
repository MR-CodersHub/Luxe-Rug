Our Heritage

Preserving Fine Art Underfoot

Founded in 2001, LuxeRug was established with a singular vision: to protect, restore, and preserve the magnificent craftsmanship of hand-knotted textiles. What started as a small local workshop has grown into a world-class conservation studio.

Every oriental, Persian, silk, and antique rug represents weeks, months, or even years of an artisan’s life. We treat each carpet not merely as a home accessory, but as an heirloom of high cultural and sentimental value.

Our facility utilizes pH-balanced organic cleansing formulas, soft flowing wash pools, and humidity-controlled drying chambers to replicate traditional outdoor washing while guaranteeing safety from structural shrinkage or color runs.
