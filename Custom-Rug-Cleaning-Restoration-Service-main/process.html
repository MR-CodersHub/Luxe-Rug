How We Clean

Time-Tested Artisan Protocols

Unlike standard steam cleaners who run high-heat machines over delicate rugs, we employ traditional submersive hand-washing methods that protect structural fibers and prevent color bleeding.

Every oriental, Persian, and custom area rug has unique traits. Our certified master weavers examine the knot structure, dye chemistry, and pile material to construct an individual cleaning plan.

Detailed Workflow

The Journey of Your Rug

From initial analysis to final delivery, we handle every textile with custom conservation care.

01

Fiber Audit

We test dye stability, document pre-existing wear, measure fringe strength, and identify specific stains to customize the wash treatment.

02

Air Dusting

Using specialized harmonic air-vibrators, we dislodge abrasive sand particles and dry soils locked deep within the pile before wetting.

03

Submersive Wash

Rugs are hand-washed in a soft-water pool with organic, pH-balanced soap, simulating natural river washing with meticulous brushing.

04

Restoration

Certified weavers secure frayed edges, re-stitch fringe margins, and fix structural holes or pile losses with wool or silk fibers.

05

Flat Drying

Rugs dry flat in a temperature and humidity-controlled chamber, preventing color run, pile deformation, or mold development.

06

Quality Audit

We hand-fringe, groom the pile direction, perform vacuum tests, and apply protective moth or soil repellent shields.
